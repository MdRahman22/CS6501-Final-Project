Lean Motion Model

State: target position, target velocity, missile position, missile velocity.

position(t): implemented by targetPositionAt and missilePositionAt.

velocity(t): implemented by targetVelocityAt and missileVelocityAt.

acceleration(t): implemented as change in velocity from one time step to the next.

Main theorem: prefixStates_eq_simulate proves the finite animation frames agree with the time-indexed model.
